Our Story

Hearth & Batter was born from a passion for creating delicious treats using only the finest natural ingredients. What started as weekend baking for family and friends quickly grew into something more as word spread about our delectable creations.

Every product is carefully crafted in small batches to ensure quality and freshness. We never use artificial preservatives, flavors, or colors in our baked goods. Our mission is simple: to bring joy through honest, homemade treats that remind you of the comfort of home.

Whether you're an experienced baker using our premixes or enjoying our ready-to-eat products, we promise an experience that's both delicious and wholesome.
